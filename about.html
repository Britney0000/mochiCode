About me

Hi there! I’m a developer with a passion for learning and a dream of building beautiful websites.
Every day, I challenge myself to improve my coding skills and bring creative ideas to life through web development. But coding isn’t my only passion! I’m also a huge K-drama and anime lover. From breathtaking adventures to heartwarming friendships, these stories inspire me with their depth, emotions, and stunning storytelling. Through MochiCode, I want to share my favorite dramas and anime with fellow fans and create a cozy space where we can all enjoy these amazing worlds together!

Whether you're here for coding, K-dramas, or anime, I hope you find something that makes you smile. Feel free to explore, share your thoughts, and let’s make this journey fun together!

Want to connect or have recommendations? Visit the Contact page and drop me a message!

A quote that inspires me

"Falling down is a way for you to see another world, a world you have never seen before."

If you’ve ever struggled to find your path or figure out what truly excites you, don’t give up. I hope to encourage you to keep going, keep exploring, and never stop chasing what you love. That’s why I created MochiCode—a space where my love for K-dramas and anime meets my passion for coding. I hope my journey inspires you to embrace your passions and create something amazing too!
